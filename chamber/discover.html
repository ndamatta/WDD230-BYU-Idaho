Discover Funes!

Funes, a charming city located in the province of Santa Fe, Argentina, is known for its tranquility, scenic beauty, and growing popularity as a residential area. This page provides an overview of the city's demographics and statistical information, along with pictures of the city.

Population

Funes has experienced notable population growth in recent years, fueled by its attractive suburban lifestyle and proximity to the city of Rosario. As of the latest available data, the city's population stands at approximately 23,520. This reflects a significant increase compared to previous years, demonstrating Funes' appeal as a residential destination. The population density is estimated to be 235.2 /km¬≤ per square kilometer, indicating a relatively low-density environment.

Geographic

Nestled in the picturesque landscape of the Pampas region, Funes enjoys a scenic setting with gently rolling hills and lush greenery. The city is located 17.5 kilometers South of Rosario, making it easily accessible for commuting and enjoying the amenities of a larger urban center. Funes benefits from a pleasant climate, characterized by mild winters and warm summers, further enhancing its desirability as a place to live.

Age Distribution

The age distribution in Funes portrays a balanced demographic structure. The city attracts residents from various age groups, offering a family-friendly environment and catering to diverse needs. While the exact figures may vary, a significant proportion of the population falls within the age range of 30 to 50 years, indicating a thriving middle-aged segment. Additionally, Funes is home to a growing number of young families, drawn to its tranquility and access to quality education.

Economic Profile

Funes primarily serves as a residential area, with many of its residents commuting to nearby cities for employment opportunities. The city's economy is closely tied to the broader economic activities of the region, particularly the city of Rosario. While Funes itself may have limited major industries, it benefits from its proximity to commercial centers and the opportunities they present.

Education and Services

Funes places great emphasis on education and provides its residents with access to quality educational institutions. The city is home to several schools, both public and private, offering education from early childhood to secondary levels. Residents also have convenient access to healthcare facilities, including medical centers and clinics, ensuring their well-being and providing necessary services.

Cultural

Funes offers a range of recreational and cultural activities to its residents. The city features parks, green spaces, and recreational areas where residents can engage in sports, leisurely walks, or family picnics. Funes also hosts local festivals, community events, and cultural activities throughout the year, fostering a sense of community and enhancing the quality of life for its residents.
