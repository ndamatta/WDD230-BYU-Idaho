Target Audience

We are focusing in business professionals and thought leaders who are currently involved in large or small businesses or who want to conduct general business in the Funes region

Target Personas

  • Members and Business Owner: People who run a business in the Funes region and are already part of the Chamber.
  • Non-members and Business Owner: People who run a business in the Funes region, but are not members of the Chamber of Commerce.
  • General Public: People who are interested in learning more about what a Chamber of Commerce is and the benefits of joining one.

Scenarios for website visitors

  • User looking for information on how to join the Chamber, costs and address.
  • User looking for upcoming events and the agenda.
  • User seeking information about the purpose and benefits of being part of the Chamber.
  • User looking for contact information to speak with a supervisor. Phone number, email, available hours.
  • User looking for pictures of the meetings and activities, looking for social media.
